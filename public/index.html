About PrivaChatApp

How to use

  • Create a free account by clicking Register and choosing a username and password of at least 6 characters.
  • Sign in with your credentials on the Sign In tab.
  • To start a conversation, type the exact username of the person you want to chat with in the search bar and press Enter.
  • Both users must have an account — share your username with people you want to chat with.
  • Messages are delivered instantly in real time.
  • To delete a message you sent, tap the 🗑 trash icon next to it — it disappears for both users immediately.
  • Keep your password safe! Losing your password means permanently losing access to your account. PrivaChatApp does not use or ask for an email address, so there is no way to reset your password.

Privacy

  • All conversations are strictly private — only you and the other person can read your messages.
  • No one else, including the app administrator, can access your conversations.
  • Unread messages are never automatically deleted — they wait until the recipient reads them.
  • Read messages are automatically and permanently deleted after 10 days.
  • Your password is securely encrypted and never stored in plain text.
  • No personal data is collected or shared with third parties.
